1st Period

The Warrior series takes place in a forest in England. In the forest there lives four clans, the WindClan, ThunderClan, RiverClan, and ShadowClan. Every clan has the same basic honor code but has different hunting and social rules. The main focus is on ThunderClan because their medicine cat, like a shaman, and their leader has had a vision of a cat with fur of fire will join the clan and unite the clans from danger.

Enter Rusty, housepet kitten, who is interested in the forest beyond his yard. One day Rusty enters the forest and becomes enamored with the life of a clan cat. The series follows his journey as an apprentince to eventually becoming leader and the difficulties and life of a clan cat.
